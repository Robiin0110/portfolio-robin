Hi, my name is Robin.
I am a Cologne based UX Designer working on the heyOBI app.

I currently work at OBI Next in Cologne, Germany. Within an interdisciplinary team, I am responsible for the UX and UI Design of the heyOBI app and other related products. In my role, I constantly work with different product owners and developers in order to build new features and transform existing ones. My business studies and my UX Design course at CareerFoundry enable me to understand business goals and transform them into user- friendly features and products.
